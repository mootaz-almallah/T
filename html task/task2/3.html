FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association) is the global governing body of football, founded in 1904. It organizes major international competitions, most notably the FIFA World Cup, held every four years. FIFA's mission is to promote football worldwide, ensure fair play, and develop the sport, including women's football. It also governs the rules of the game and supports national federations with development programs. Despite facing criticism for corruption issues, FIFA remains central to the global football community.
